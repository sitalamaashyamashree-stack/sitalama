About Us

Who We Are

Shyamashree Sitala Maa Charitable Trust is a non-profit organization dedicated to uplifting underprivileged communities through education, healthcare, women empowerment, and cultural development. We work tirelessly to bring positive change in the lives of those who need it the most.

Our Vision

To build a compassionate society where every individual has access to essential resources, dignity, and opportunities to grow.

Our Mission

  • Supporting education for underprivileged children.
  • Providing healthcare assistance to needy families.
  • Empowering women through skills and training.
  • Organizing community welfare and awareness programs.
  • Promoting sustainable rural development.

Our Values

  • Transparency in all activities.
  • Compassion for all without discrimination.
  • Integrity in every action and decision.
  • Long-term impact through sustainability.
